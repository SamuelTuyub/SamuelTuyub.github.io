"Sounds" Project Part 1

These works center around exploring the possibilities of sound and its impact on our perception of the world around us. Through a series of videos, I have delved into the intricate details of sound and how it interacts with our environment. Specifically, I have been fascinated by the way sounds are distributed across the left and right channels of stereo audio, and the unique effects this can have on our perception of a given sound or piece of music. By experimenting with different sound arrangements and recording techniques, I have created a body of work that challenges the listener's expectations and encourages them to engage with sound in a new and exciting way. Through my work, I hope to inspire a deeper appreciation for the complexities of sound and its role in shaping our experience of the world.

"Sounds" Project Part 2

Final

This work revolves around creating eerie and unsettling experiences through digital media. This is captured in a digital environment, which draws inspiration from the horror film genre. The video is carefully crafted to replicate the elements that make a horror film so frightening, such as eerie sound effects, suspenseful pacing, and haunting imagery. Through this work, I try to explore the ways in which digital technology can be used to create immersive and emotionally charged experiences, and to challenge the boundaries between the physical and digital worlds.
